Akanksha Mamidiga does Chekov tonight

THE ANNIVERSARY by Anton Chekov
KHIRIN.

Send out to the chemist for 15 copecks' worth of valerian drops, and tell them to bring some drinking water into the Directors' office!

[_The private office of the Chairman of Directors. On the left is a door, leading into the public department. There are two desks._]

SHIPUCHIN.

At the door, addresses the outer office] This present, my dear colleagues, will be preserved to the day of my death, as a memory of the happiest days of my life!

[_Noise and applause behind the scenes. SHIPUCHIN'S voice: "Thank you! Thank you! I am extremely grateful." Enter SHIPUCHIN. He wears a frockcoat and white tie; he carries an album which has been just presented to him._]

KHIRIN.

I have the honour to congratulate you, Andrey Andreyevitch, on the fiftieth anniversary of our Bank, and hope that...

[_I have the honour to congratulate you, Andrey Andreyevitch, on the fiftieth anniversary of our Bank, and hope that...._]

SHIPUCHIN.
SHIPUCHIN.

Thank you, my dear sir! Thank you! I think that in view of the unique character of the day, as it is an anniversary, we may kiss each other!

[_[Sighs] Yes, fifteen years! Fifteen years as my name's Shipuchin! [Changes his tone] Where's my report? Is it getting on?_]

KHIRIN.

Yes; there's only five pages left..

[_If nothing occurs to disturb me, I'll get it done. Nothing of any importance is now left. _]

SHIPUCHIN.

Splendid. Splendid, as my name's Shipuchin! The general meeting will be at four.

[_Another unpleasantness.... This morning your wife came to see me and complained about you once again. Said that last night you threatened her and her sister with a knife. Kusma Nicolaievitch, what do you mean by that? Oh, oh!._]
